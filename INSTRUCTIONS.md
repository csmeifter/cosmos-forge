# 📖 Celestial Frontier — Game Instructions

> **Your complete guide to building a thriving space colony!**

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Step 1: Launch the Game
- Open `index.html` in any modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)
- No installation or internet required — it runs entirely offline!

### Step 2: Name Your Colony
- Type a name for your colony in the text field (e.g., "Nova Prime", "Starlight Base")
- This is YOUR colony — make the name legendary! ✨

### Step 3: Choose Your Destination
Pick one of four real celestial bodies to colonize:

| Destination | Difficulty | Why It's Special |
|-------------|-----------|------------------|
| 🔴 **Mars** | ⭐⭐ Moderate | Thin atmosphere, polar ice caps, 24h 37m day — the most Earth-like option |
| 🌑 **The Moon** | ⭐⭐⭐ Hard | No atmosphere, craters with ice deposits, only 3 days from Earth |
| 🧊 **Europa** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Expert | Jupiter's icy moon with 2× Earth's ocean water hidden under ice |
| 🌫️ **Titan** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ Expert | Saturn's largest moon with methane lakes and a thick nitrogen atmosphere |

> 💡 **Tip for beginners**: Start with **Mars** — it has the best balance of available resources!

### Step 4: Launch!
Click the **🚀 Launch Colony** button to begin your mission.

## 🎮 How to Play

### The Dashboard
Once you launch, you'll see your colony dashboard with:

- **📊 Resource Bars** (top) — Shows your Oxygen, Water, Energy, Food & Minerals levels
  - 🟢 Green = Healthy (above 60%)
  - 🟡 Yellow = Caution (30-60%)
  - 🔴 Red = Critical (below 30%) — take action immediately!
- **👥 Population Counter** — How many colonists live in your colony
- **😊 Morale Meter** — How happy your colonists are
- **☀️ Sol Counter** — What day it is (Sol = one day on your celestial body)
- **📜 Event Log** — Shows what happened each turn

### Building Your Colony

1. **Look at the Building Panel** on the left side of the screen
2. **Click on a building card** to select it — you'll see its cost and what it does
3. **Click on an empty tile** on the colony grid to place it
4. The building cost (minerals + energy) will be deducted from your resources

### Advancing Time

- Click the **⏭️ Next Sol** button to advance to the next day
- Each Sol (turn), the following happens:
  1. 🏭 Buildings **produce** resources (energy, food, water, oxygen, minerals)
  2. 👥 Colonists **consume** resources (oxygen, water, food, energy)
  3. 📈 Population **grows** if you have enough housing, food, water, and oxygen
  4. 🎲 A **random event** might occur (see below)
  5. 😊 Colony **morale** is recalculated

## 🏆 How to Win

**Goal**: Reach a population of **100 colonists** with:
- ✅ Morale at **50% or above**
- ✅ No critical resources at zero

### 🗺️ Winning Strategy (Step by Step)

1. **Sols 1-5**: Build 2 Mining Facilities and 2 Solar Arrays for resource production
2. **Sols 5-10**: Add Water Extractor, Oxygen Generator, and a Greenhouse
3. **Sols 10-15**: Build your first Habitat Module to grow population
4. **Sols 15-25**: Add more Greenhouses and resource buildings to support growth
5. **Sols 25-35**: Build a Research Lab and start unlocking upgrades
6. **Sols 35-50**: Build Shield Generators to protect your colony
7. **Sols 50+**: Scale up Habitats and food/water/oxygen to reach 100 colonists!

### ❌ How to Lose
- 💀 Population drops to **0**
- ⚠️ Oxygen, Water, or Food stays at **0 for 3 consecutive Sols**

## 💡 Pro Tips & Strategies

1. 🏗️ **Always build resource buildings before Habitats** — more colonists = more consumption
2. ⚡ **Energy is king** — almost everything needs energy, so build plenty of Solar Arrays
3. 🛡️ **Shield Generators are worth it** — they prevent costly building damage
4. 📊 **Watch the yellow/red warnings** — fix resource shortages before they become critical
5. 🔬 **Research early** — upgrades compound over time and make the late game much easier
6. 🌍 **Read the science facts** — the educational panel teaches you real space science!
7. ⚖️ **Balance is key** — don't over-invest in one resource at the expense of others
8. 🧊 **Adapt to your location** — each celestial body has different strengths and weaknesses

## 🌍 Real Science Behind the Game

This game is based on real space colonization research:

- **MOXIE** (Mars Oxygen ISRU Experiment) — NASA tested making oxygen from Mars's CO₂ atmosphere on the Perseverance rover
- **Regolith Mining** — Scientists plan to extract minerals from lunar/Martian soil for construction
- **Hydroponics** — Growing food without soil is already used on the ISS
- **Solar Energy in Space** — Solar panels work differently depending on distance from the Sun
- **Europa Clipper** — NASA's real mission launching to study Europa's subsurface ocean
- **Dragonfly** — NASA's drone mission heading to Titan to explore its surface
